Glow

Long-Distance Touch Lamp

╲╱

Roles: Marketer, Product Designer

Our goal with Glow was to create a meaningful, yet simple solution to bridge the gap between anyone with a distance. I worked on creating ideas for iterations for our lamp product as well as develop our marketing aspects.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Sketch, Fusion 360, Final Cut Pro



Problem

Too many people face consequences from being distanced -- that may be military parents and children; physically-distanced best friends; or long-distance relationships.

Studies have shown that military parents' inability to interact with their family hurts the familial dynamic and hurts the military parents' ability to focus on work-related tasks.


Solution

Glow is a breakthrough lineup of interactive lamps connecting people all across the world through touch. Our mission is to illuminate the lives of the distanced, one drawing at a time. We serve to provide a new meaningful form of connection that is unattainable in any existing medium or technological modality.



Prototype


When you draw on one Glow Lamp, the drawing appears on your loved one’s Glow Lamp across the world; users have the ability to send live doodles to each other with Glow’s interactive canvas.

*The logo and storyboard were created by my teammates, but were included to display Glow's usabiltiy.
