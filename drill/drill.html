Drill

Workout Timer App

╲╱

Roles: Branding Manager, UI/UX Designer

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I embarked on a journey of consistent exercise as a means of releasing pent up energy during the quarantine period. I realized that there was no free workout timer app that contained all the needed features I wanted in my fitness journey. So, I decided to create my own workout timer app. I branded, designed, and prototyped a timer app named Drill.

Programs Used: Figma

Problem

When looking at the current market, there is no completely free workout timer that includes all the needed features to build the perfect exercise routine. While some free timer apps encompass methodologies for crafting a workout, the apps lack the ability to track the progress and consistency of routines.



Solution

Drill is an open source fully comprehensive workout timer app. When developing Drill, I had a large focus on customization and aesthetic as these were things that were missing in every free workout timer app on the current market.


In terms of the timer itself, I included core necessities of what I noticed I wanted in the perfect workout timer.


I also wanted to make sure I had a calendar within the app to track workouts and statistics.
