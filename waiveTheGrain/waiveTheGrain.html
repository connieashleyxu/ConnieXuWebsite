Waive the Grain

Healthy Korean-French Bakery

╲╱

Roles: Branding Lead

After being a part of the college community for a couple of months, I realized how much food incentivizes the students. Therefore, I created Waive the Grain Bakery and developed branding for the bakery.

Roles: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator

Problem

Most of the food that is offered on college campuses are unhealthy and not always the most appetizing.


Solution

I had the idea of creating a Korean-French fusion bakery to provide a unique take on healthier and tastier options for students who are looking for a quick bite.
