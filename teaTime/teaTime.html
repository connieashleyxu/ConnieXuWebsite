The Problem

While the website has fun interactive aspects, we wanted to ensure we were advocating for a positive impact in our society.

In the US, more than 500 million plastic straws are used and discarded each day. Because straws are extremely lightweight and made of polyprophylene plastic, they cannot be recycled and often end up in the wrong places. As one of the most common ocean pollution, many of the straws we use end up in our earth's oceans where they do not biodegrade or dissolve, but rather break down into increasingly smaller pieces known as microplastics. These microplastics end up contaminating our oceans in addition to harming our marine life. By the year 2050 the amount of plastic in the ocean is set to increase tenfold 99% of all sea birds will have ingested plastic there will be more plastic in the ocean than fish.

The Solution

Tea Time aims to bring awareness to the #SaveTheTurtles movement as well as a more general understanding of the benefits of tea. It was also important to us to have more fun and light-hearted elements in the website.
