Q&A Discussion

Q. How will you combat the willingness to pay from Gen Zers?
From our survey, we can tell that a large number of users are unwilling to pay a one-time fee of $0.99 for a social media app. However, more users would pay for a subscription model for enhanced features in Harmonize. For example, premium members would have unlimited swipes or an ad free expereince. We see many other apps using the same freemium model (e.i Tinder and Spotify).

Q. How will you combat cooperators like Spotify and Apple Music turning into competitors if you are utilizing their APIs?
Currently, both Spotify and Apple Music have their open source APIs that are meant for creatives and developers to tinker around with the possibilities of said data. While Harmonize using their APIs at the moment for our matching algorithm, upon further expansion, we hope to develop our mathching algorithm to be an entirely in-house solution.
