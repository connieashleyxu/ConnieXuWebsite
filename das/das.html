A couple of friends and I created the Daily Awareness Societty (DAS) as a means to educate ourselves and others in our community about pertinent issues that are not discussed enough. I branded, designed, coded the website, and created content for our organization.

This blog is also a source to just spill out my thoughts through writing.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator, Figma

Coding Lanuage: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Problem

Too often, we find ourselves stuck inside a bubble shielded from the fast-paced world outside of our comfort zones. We often neglect important world events, issues around us, and even our own well-being.

Solution

With the power of social media, it's easier than ever to reach a large audience in hopes of promoting education of topics that aren't covered in American core education.

DAS is a collective blog with a focus on social awareness.

Originally, DAS held in-person meetings in our local North Texas community to discuss various topics with our peers. However, we have been transitioning to a greater virtual presence in order to bring our ideas to a broader audience.
