Problem

Too often, we find ourselves stuck inside a bubble shielded from the fast-paced world outside of our comfort zones. We often neglect important world events, issues around us, and even our own well-being.



Solution

With the power of social media, it's easier than ever to reach a large audience in hopes of promoting education of topics that aren't covered in American core education.

DAS is a collective blog with a focus on social awareness. Check out the website at www.dailyawarenesssociety.com

Originally, DAS held in-person meetings in our local North Texas community to discuss various topics with our peers. However, we have been transitioning to a greater virtual presence in order to bring our ideas to a broader audience.

The Daily Awareness Society (DAS) began as an organization at the University of North Texas (UNT) where students could join in on live discussions of pertinent topics happening not only on the national news, but also in the school.



User Testing I

From our Facebook group, we received a tremendous amount of positive feedback from our members!

Due to the novel Coronavirus, it became difficult to uphold our regular in-person meetings. Therefore, our team decided to digitalize DAS with a blog website so that our members can still stay active and engaged.

User Testing II

Utilizing Google Analytics on our blog site, we were able to identify that most of our members read our articles through a mobile device. Therefore, we wanted to emphasize adaptability for viewing DAS on the mobile-end.

From engagement APIs embedded into the DAS site and on Instagram, we are seeing a positive trend in viewers and interactions.



Marketing

Social Media







Social Media

To increase promotional avenues, our team decided to gradually migrate from Facebook to Instagram by creating the @dailyawarenesssociety Instagram page.

On our Instagram, we post promotional reminders to view our weekly articles. Beyond promotions, we like to keep our users engaged and interested by posting daily news to our story as well as interactive posts to allow mainstreamed user feedback.
