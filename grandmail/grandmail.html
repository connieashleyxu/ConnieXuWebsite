Grandmail

Cross-Generational Subscription Box

╲╱

Roles: Branding

With the goal of promoting well-being not only within the COVID-19 era, but beyond the pandemic as well, our goal was to connect generations through interactive communication-based tasks. We created a subscription box model, Grandmail, that would allow grandparents and older grandchildren develop a deeper emotional bond with one another. I worked with a partner on the logo design and package design aspect of branding for Grandmail.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Procreate



The Problem

We all have that moment when we realize we forgot to call Grandma and/or Grandpa -- that feeling of guilt is what we are trying to fix. We’re all missing out on something important!


The Solution

Grandmail is a monthly subscription service that provides engaging communication-based activities to build memorabilia between grandparents and grandchildren. Our go-to market targets older children as a means to develop a more meaningful and mature relationships.
