Mindful Minutes

Counting App

╲╱

Roles: Branding Manager, UI/UX Designer, App Developer, Researcher

As winners of the 2019 Congressional App Challenge, my partner and I wanted to create an app to combat a problem we have noticed to have peaked in the digital age: forgetfulness. We developed a counting app, Mindful Minutes, to allow our universal market to keep track of anything. I researched branded, designed, and coded the app.

Programs Used: Adobe Illustrator

Coding Language: JavaScript, Swift

Problem

In our fast-paced society, it’s easy for work, play, and health to jumble into a blur. Research has proven that the average human’s attention span is a mere 8 seconds. It’s no wonder we often forget how many calories we have eaten or how many times we have gone to the gym for the month. It's too difficult for us to keep track of all the modalities we have in our lives.



Solution

We created a habit counting app that promotes positive thinking and an organized lifestyle. From our color palette to the design of the interface, every aspect of Mindful Minutes has the purpose of creating the optimal environment for our users to have a fully functional lifestyle.
