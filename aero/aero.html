Introduction

Enlisted by the creators of Aero to enhance their branding, our team worked to develop a mock up for the Aero app as well as build a brand behind Aero.

Aero's team wanted to connect the different talent pools and projects at USC through the use of an app. Their ultimate goal is to give college students the confidence and resources they need to explore their potential and build their personal portfolio.

Q&A Discussion

Q. What is the meaning behind the space theme?
Honoring our client's wishes for keeping a space theme, we created a series of lexicons for Aero. Our goal was to emulate metaphors with all of our creations. For example, the revamped logo focuses on loops and rockets to display not only interconnectedness, but also the idea that Aero will help launch startups. Moreover, our mascot (Rover) has softer features with space themes to generate a feeling of helpfulness for Aero users.
