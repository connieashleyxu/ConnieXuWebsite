Roles: Branding Lead, UI/UX Designer

Enlisted by the creators of Aero to enhance their branding, I worked to develop a mock up for the Aero app as well as build a brand behind Aero.

Programs Used: Procreate, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD



Problem

Aero's team wanted to connect the different talent pools and projects at USC through the use of an app. Their ultimate goal is to give college students the confidence and resources they need to explore their potential and build their personal portfolio.

However, Aero's team needed help with solidfying their company's branding structure.


Solution

Our team worked with Aero to build the company's brand integreity through developing icons and lexicons to better fit the brand's desired aesthetic.
