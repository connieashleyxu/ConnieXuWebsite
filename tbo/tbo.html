Idea

The TAMS Business Organization (TBO) aims to involve the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science (TAMS) and University of North Texas (UNT) student population in entrepreneurship, business competitions, as well as networking opportunities in order to prepare them for career opportunities. Our goal is to stimulate professionalism, leaderhip, communication, and ethics in our community.

In order to get the larger student body of TAMS and UNT invested in the organization, branding was critical.
