Collaborative Virtual Workplace

╲╱

Roles: Branding, UI/UX

Presented at the Iovine and Young Academy. In lieu of the theme of collaboration for different groups of people, I worked with a team of creators to build a virtual playground for college students to innovate, disrupt, and collaborate named Collaborative.ly. My roles included developing the ideas of the project, creating the landing page for collaborative.ly, and delve into market feasibility.

Programs Used: Adobe XD, Adobe InDesign



Problem

There is no college-friendly virtual workspace where students can find projects to work on. Most interactive platforms on the current market make it too difficult to reach out to others, require the use of too many platforms, and lack a creative spark.


Solution

Collaborative.ly is a fully customizable web platform that provides students with a digital makerspace to share work and collaborate on projects to promote collaboration between students outside the virtual walls of a Zoom classroom.
