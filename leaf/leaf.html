Leaf

Educational Web App

╲╱

Roles: Project Manager (Illustrator, UI/UX Designer, Web Developer, and Business Analyst)

As winners of the University of Chicago's Francis Yuen East Asia 2020 Hackathon, our team created a web app to innovate a solution to climate change by mapping personal carbon emissions.

Programs Used: Figma, Procreate, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects

Coding Language: HTML, CSS, JS, Python



Problem

Hack Challenge: Innovate a solution to climate change by mapping personal carbon emissions.

Our team saw huge market opportunities in the emerging e-learning field for younger children.


Solution

Our team worked to create Leaf, an educational web app tailored towards children (age 9+) in efforts to mitigate the effects of carbon emissions starting with a new generation.
